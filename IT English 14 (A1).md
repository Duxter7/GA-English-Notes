
To be = быть; являться
	am
		I **am** busy
			I'm busy
		I **am** sleepy
			I'm sleepy
	are
		They **are** hungry
			They're hungry 
		You **are** angry
			You're angry
	is
		He **is** hungry
		She isn't angry
		It
Negatives
	**am** not = aren't = 're not
	**are** not = 'm not
Questions
	**Are** you busy?
	**Am** I sleepy?
Short answers
	Are you busy?
		Yes, I am
		No, I am not
